Moderation Policy

Transparent by design. Last updated: May 19, 2026

Our Commitment

XenChee does not shadowban. Every moderation action is logged publicly below. If content is removed you are told why. Every decision can be appealed.

What Gets Removed

We remove only content that crosses legal or absolute ethical lines:

  • CSAM — Child sexual abuse material. Immediate removal and NCMEC report. No exceptions.
  • Credible specific threats — Explicit threats of imminent violence against named individuals.
  • Doxxing — Publishing private personal information of others without consent.
  • Illegal content — Content that violates applicable federal law.

What We Do Not Remove

We do not remove content based on viewpoint, political opinion, unpopular speech, offensive language, controversial topics, or content that mainstream platforms would suppress. Adults are capable of deciding for themselves what to read and engage with.

The Strike System

Violations result in strikes against your account.

  • Strike 1 — Warning and content removal. Reason provided.
  • Strike 2 — Temporary posting restriction. Reason provided.
  • Strike 3 — Account review. Possible suspension.
  • CSAM — Immediate permanent ban. No strikes. NCMEC report filed.

Appeals

Every moderation decision can be appealed. Email info@xenchee.com with your username and the content in question. Appeals are reviewed within 72 hours. Appeal outcomes are final and logged publicly.
