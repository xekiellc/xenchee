What is XenChee?

XenChee is a free speech maximalist social platform for verified adults. We built it because we believe adults have the right to speak freely, connect authentically, and participate in communities without algorithmic manipulation or arbitrary censorship.

Why XenChee?

The major social platforms have become increasingly restrictive — shadowbanning users, suppressing viewpoints, and making moderation decisions without transparency or accountability. We believe there is a better way. XenChee is that better way.

Our Principles

No shadowbans — ever. If your content is removed you will always be told why. We do not quietly suppress your reach. Every moderation action is documented in our public moderation log.

Chronological feed. You see posts in the order they were posted. We do not use algorithms to decide what you see or what gets suppressed. The feed is yours.

Verified adults only. XenChee is an 18+ platform. Age verification at signup ensures every voice here belongs to a real adult.

Your data is yours. We do not sell your data. We do not build advertising profiles. We collect only what is necessary to operate the platform.

Transparent moderation. Our moderation decisions are public. Every removal, every strike, every appeal outcome is logged and visible. We have nothing to hide.

The Ecosystem

XenChee is part of a larger ecosystem of platforms built for audiences the mainstream internet underserves. Our communities span politics, science, sports, entertainment, crypto, and more — each one a home for voices that deserve to be heard.
