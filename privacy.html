Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2026

1. What We Collect

When you create an account we collect your email address, username, and date of birth. When you use the platform we collect posts, comments, reactions, and other content you create. We collect standard server logs including IP addresses and browser information.

2. What We Do Not Collect

We do not collect payment information. We do not track your activity across other websites. We do not build advertising profiles. We do not use third-party tracking pixels or advertising SDKs.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to operate the platform — to display your posts, connect you with other users, and maintain your account. We use your email address to send account-related communications. We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

4. We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for commercial purposes. Period.

5. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase infrastructure hosted in the United States. We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account we will delete your personal data within 30 days. Content you have posted may remain visible unless you delete it before account deletion.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access your personal data, correct inaccurate data, and request deletion of your account and associated data. To exercise these rights contact us at info@voxxee.com.

8. CSAM Reporting

We are legally required to report child sexual abuse material to the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC). Any such content is immediately removed and reported. This is a legal obligation we take seriously.

9. Cookies

We use essential cookies only — those necessary to keep you logged in and maintain your session. We do not use advertising cookies or tracking cookies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify users of material changes. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

Privacy questions may be directed to info@voxxee.com
